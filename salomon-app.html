Project overview

Objectives / Context / Challenge & Goal / Design process

False but quite plausible

Salomon is one of my favorite brands because of its design and quality. Therefore, I prepared a brief for a new product/service based on it. I also added my experience with clients in various industries. Here is a summary:

Objectives

Creating a tool different from those offered in the market by competitors so that you can sell/present products non-intrusively. It must integrate all sports for which the company produces sports equipment.

Context

Rivals are launching sales apps to simplify shopping. Customers trust established brands for quality and variety in sports equipment. They also compare prices and specs on search sites for the best options.

Requirments

Developing a native app merges company expertise and diverse products for seamless, efficient sports equipment shopping. No extra staffing might be needed.

An idea & my place in it

As per explodingtopics.com, about 6.84 billions smartphones are used globally, roughly 85% of the 8 billion population. Our focus will be on smartphone users. What next - new website or additional pages on the existing web? No, and No. Salomon already have it (on the example of snowboarding)

So the client wants
  • something new,
  • something that will combine "showing off" knowledge and experience in sports equipment,
  • something that will support sales,
  • widely available - native app.
My proposition
Native application combining the selection of equipment with the possibility of its purchase. Taking advantage of the ability to automatically configure equipment for a particular sport will be a tempting option for a potential customer. This is indicated by two things:
  • the number of sites having this thread (over 6 millions),
  • and the time it takes to read articles on equipment selection (on one page only) - from 40 min to 90 min.

Challenge & Goal

On the basis of research, observations and preliminary information, I defined the basic challenge, goal and set a thesis for the solution.

My role: UX/UI designer
Tools: Figma / Balsamiq / Photoshop
Context: Project of the CareerFoundry UI course
Main Challenge

modern design - maintaining readability and accessibility expandability as a business objective (a structure that allows you to add more sports)

Solution

use the classic, dark mode - verification of the contrast at each stage of the project simplification user path to configuration - simple architecture & flow

Main Goal

replace description from the website with a single tool build a tool to help customers quickly and easily select equipment for themselves structure that allows to add more sports

Design Process

In this project, I tried to keep all the following steps by the Design Thinking methodology.

DISCOVER & DEFINE
How to find the right Solution?
  • explore the problem & decide what to fix
    • analysis of the existence of a similar solution
  • interviews / user testing
DEVELOPE & DELIVER
How to solve the problem in the right way?
  • test potential solution & refine final solution
  • user testing
  • iterating
