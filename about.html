In a nutshell

As a freelancer and agency graphic designer, my ultimate goal is to bring the client's vision to life, no matter the constraints. Innovation and creativity were my tools to surpass expectations and deliver captivating results. I have worked for 8 different industries, preparing all kinds of marketing and branding materials.

Engineering education, over 4 years in customer service, and over 8 years as a visual designer - my professional career is a bit of luck and a lot of hard work.

My recipe for being a good employee?
A high work ethic, sincere commitment, truthfulness, and honesty built the opinion that I am trustworthy and reliable with creative sparkle.

Since 2015, I have been working as a visual designer and developed a lot of skills, but I do not stop there. I continue to learn and grow just like the world around me. I am up to date with the news cheering the development of AI and the automation of designers' work.
